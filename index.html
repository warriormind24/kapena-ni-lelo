About us

Rooted in Luanshya, built for reliable supply

We are a privately owned commercial poultry farm contributing to Zambia’s food production through responsible, profitable operations — with customers’ interests at the centre of everything we do.

01

To become one of the leading commercial poultry farm brands in Zambia and on the regional stage.

02

Mission

To manage the company efficiently for customers, shareholders, employees, contractors, and the community — with truth, fairness, and benevolence.

03

How we operate

Qualified, creative teams; performance-based leadership; and governance aligned with our board of trustees and best practices in commercial poultry farming.
